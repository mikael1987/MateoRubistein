"Buried Lives" on tour on 2021.

January, 2021.

The play"Buried Lives" is coming back on tour by the end of the year and in the beggining of 2021. Buried Lives will be performed in Valencia, Seville, Vigo, Ourense, Ávila and Logroño, among other spanish cities.
Mateo Rubistein plays the role of Primitivo Florián in this breathtaking play about memory, love and loss, written by Laila Ripoll, Pepe Viyuela, Mafalda Bellido, Alfonso Plou, Juanjo Millás and Juan Mayorga.

"El Hijo", already available on Filmin.

August, 2020.
Available from this month the medium-length film "El Hijo" for viewing on Filmin. Mateo Rubistein plays a leading role in this story with Ignacio Montes, Pedro Casablanc and Fanny de Castro .

"El Hijo" was recorded in Malaga under the direction of Venci Kostov, and tells the story of Pedro, a young man whose life does not match the environment around him, nor with his family or friends.

"Buried Lives", candidate for the 2020 Max Awards.

March, 2020.
"Buried Lives", a play in which Mateo Rubistein plays the role of Primitivo, is a candidate for the XXIII edition of the Max Awards for Performing Arts.
It is competing in the category of Best Theatrical Authorship for the texts of Laila Ripoll, Juanjo Millás, Pepe Viyuela, Juan Mayorga, Alfonso Plou and Mafalda Bellido.

The tour of The Youth Company play "Barro" begins.

February, 2020.
"Barro", the latest play of The Youth Company set in World War I, will be performed in theaters such as the Gayarre Theater, in Pamplona, and in cities like Seville, Soria or Malaga.
In "Barro", Mateo Rubistein plays a French soldier named Marcel , grandson of a general, who will battle in Marne to save his comrades, while he is trying to maintain the honor of his family.

"National Anger", a new glaze at youth and diversity in the National Drama Center of Spain

April, 2019.

The inclusive scenic project "Desgana Nacional" begins, presented by Mateo Rubistein at the prestigious Centro Dramático Nacional . The project will be developed at the festival A Different Look , with the collaboration of Lucía Miranda and Angel Peralbá.
A group of 12 actors and actresses, with and without functional diversity, will investigate on stage about the difficulties and opportunities faced by young people today and they will reflect about new technologies, love, inclusion and diversity.

The Youth Company, more alive than ever

January, 2019.

"The Foundation" returns to the Conde Duque Theater and "Barro" is premiered at Teatros del Canal, both theatrical plays by The Youth Company in which participates Mateo Rubistein .
"The Foundation", by Buero Vallejo, presents a group of young people who face a common destiny, discovering that nothing is what it seems to be. "Barro" is the first part of the tetralogy "Map of the ruins of Europe", a set of plays that will deal with the most important events of our continent.

"Four Hearts with Brake and Reverse" on Galileo Theater.

July, 2018.
"Four Hearts with Brake and Reverse", the most crazy and timeless work by Jardiel Poncela, will premiere this summer at the Galileo Theater. Gabriel Olivares ( Burundanga, Our Town, Gross Indecency ) is the director of the play, with Mateo Rubistein in his cast .
In this fun, hilarious, colorful and surreal story signed for Jardiel, two couples and a postman will drink a potion to live forever ... with all that this entails .
