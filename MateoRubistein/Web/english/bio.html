Actor since he was 15 years old, Mateo Rubistein begins his career with episodic roles in spanish TV series such as Red Eagle or The Mysteries of Laura. Since 2016 he is a part of the cast of The Youth Theatre Company, and since then he has performed continuously in some of the most prestigious theatres of Madrid, such as Teatros del Canal, National Drama Center, Galileo Theatre or Conde Duque Center. He also performed international plays in the United Kingdom (El Campamento, La habitación de Mateo) and France (Action!).
